# Game Vision

## Overview

A Street Fighter–style fighting game built in **Roblox**, inspired by **Mortal Kombat 1**. Players spawn into a shared lobby where they can hang out, socialize, and challenge each other to fights — turning every match into a personal showdown rather than a queued, anonymous matchup.

## Player Flow

1. **Lobby first.** Every player spawns into a shared lobby server where users can move around freely, see each other, and just exist in the world.
2. **Challenge-driven matches.** Fights are initiated by one player challenging another directly. There's no matchmaking queue at the core of the experience — the social act of issuing and accepting a challenge is part of the fun.
3. **Showdown.** Once a challenge is accepted, the two players drop into a fight, and the rest of the experience (combat, finishers, rewards) unfolds from there.
4. **Back to the lobby.** After the match ends, players return to the social space.

## Game Modes

- **Duels** — the core 1v1 challenge-and-fight loop described above.
- **SmashBros-style mode** _(exploratory)_ — a more chaotic multi-player party mode under consideration as a secondary offering.

## Combat Mechanics

Combat is built around a focused set of tools that every character shares, plus character-specific flavor on top:

- **Aerial, mid, and low attacks** as the standard normals.
- **Grabs** for handling defensive opponents.
- **Special attacks** that are character-dependent and define each fighter's identity.
- **Blocking** as the core defensive option.
- **Finishers** as cinematic match-ending moments.

## Combos

Combo design is still being shaped, with **aerial combos** as the first category to explore. The intent is for combos to feel expressive and player-driven rather than locked into rigid strings.

## Characters

- **Custom-animated** characters built specifically for the game.
- **Skins** unlocked through the season pass and the in-game store.
- **Animation variants** — alternate animations for special attacks, giving players a way to personalize how their favorite character looks in motion.

## Features & Progression

- **Season Pass** (free tier only for now)
  - 50 levels of progression
  - Rewards include in-game credits, skins, emotes, and characters
- **In-Game Store**
  - Sells skins, emotes, and special attack animations
- **Character Customization**
  - Skins and animation variants are the primary way players express themselves and show off investment in a character

## North Star

The game should feel like a real fighting game living inside a social Roblox lobby — where every match is a story between two players who chose to fight each other, and where long-term play is driven by mastering characters and personalizing how they look and move.
